Student & Female‑Worker Housing

Comfortable, safe living close to campus

UniBase is a modern residential building in Mabela 8 offering private, fully furnished rooms for university students and female workers who want privacy, comfort, and quick access to nearby universities.

Living at UniBase

What you get

The UniBase concept focuses on simple, practical housing: private rooms, essential shared spaces, and helpful services that make day‑to‑day life easier for residents.

Your room

  • Comfortable bed, study desk, storage and AC.

About UniBase

Our idea in brief

UniBase started as a university entrepreneurship project to solve the lack of affordable, comfortable private housing near universities in Mabela.

The vision is to provide a safe, well‑managed building where students and female workers can focus on their studies and work while enjoying a calm place to live.

Goals
What we aim for
  • Offer quality rooms at prices that fit a student budget.
  • Maintain high occupancy by staying close to universities and listening to residents’ feedback.
  • Build strong relationships with nearby universities and local service providers.
Future
Growing the concept

If the first building performs well, the UniBase model can be repeated in other high‑demand student areas such as Al Khoudh, with similar private rooms, shared spaces and services.
